1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Comprehensive Cloud Technologies Pvt Ltd ("CCT", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information through the Tap-N-Eat platform — a school meal management and cashless wallet system accessible via web portals and the Tap-N-Eat mobile application.

By using Tap-N-Eat, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform and contact your school administrator.

3. Data We Collect

Parent / Guardian Data

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Account credentials (password stored as a secure hash — never in plain text)
  • Wallet balance and top-up transaction history

Student Data

  • Student name and school-assigned student ID
  • RFID tag identifier (used for contactless meal authentication at school)
  • Meal transaction history (date, time, item, amount deducted)
  • Subscription and canteen plan details

School Administrator & Staff Data

  • Full name, username, and school-assigned credentials
  • Role and permission level within the platform

Technical & Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and network type (for security logging)
  • In-app navigation and feature usage (for platform improvement)
  • Error logs and crash reports
We do NOT collect: precise GPS location, background location, government ID, biometric data, or any financial card/bank account numbers. All payment processing is handled by Razorpay under their own privacy policy.

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Restriction — Request that we limit how we process your data
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests

7. Data Storage & International Transfers

Data is stored on servers hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in India. Where data is transferred to international sub-processors (e.g., cloud infrastructure, analytics), appropriate safeguards are in place including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions under applicable data protection law.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to any third parties for marketing purposes.

8. Security Measures

  • Passwords are stored using strong one-way cryptographic hashing (bcrypt/argon2)
  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
  • Role-based access control — school admins can only access their own school's data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access logs are maintained and monitored for anomalous activity

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to the rights and freedoms of individuals, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  • Report to relevant supervisory authorities as required under applicable data protection laws
  • Provide full details of the breach, data affected, and steps taken to mitigate harm

10. Cookies & Local Storage

The Tap-N-Eat web portal uses browser localStorage (not third-party cookies) to maintain your login session and store school branding preferences locally on your device. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

The Tap-N-Eat mobile application uses device-local secure storage for the same purpose. No data is shared with advertising networks.

11. Children's Privacy

Tap-N-Eat is designed for use in schools and processes student data only under the direction of the school (as data controller) and with parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children. Student accounts are created and managed exclusively by school administrators.

12. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account at any time by:

  • Using the account settings within the Tap-N-Eat mobile app or web portal, or
  • Emailing system.user@cctindia.in with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

All personal data will be permanently erased within 7 working days unless retention is required by law (e.g., financial records).

13. Refund & Wallet Policy

Wallet top-up amounts loaded into a student's Tap-N-Eat wallet are intended for use within the school's canteen system. Please note:

  • Wallet top-ups are processed securely via Razorpay
  • Refund eligibility for unused wallet balance is determined by the individual school's policy
  • Technical errors during payment (double-charge, payment debited but not reflected) will be resolved within 5–7 business days — contact us at system.user@cctindia.in
  • Comprehensive Cloud Technologies does not charge any platform fee to parents for using the Tap-N-Eat service
